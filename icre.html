INDIAN COMMERCIAL REAL ESTATE

Indian Commercial Real Estate has been, is and will be a very attractive investment avenue considering the resources that Indian soil offers to the humanity. However, when it comes to being organized sector and availability of [reliable] data India is still a budding nation. We have miles to achieve.

Technology has various options that can be utilized to address the above challenges. HDS has the required domain knowledge and technology solutions to make it easy for the developers and equity investors, specifically in the commercial real estate.

In store for Developers

HDS provides developers with Feasibility Analysis and development capital consultation services. Under the feasibility analysis, developers are provided with as much options and scenarios depicting the cost involved to the expected returns, based on which developers can make the appropriate decisions.

HDS also provides analysis using standard, globally used software or MS Excel for the feasibility analysis, depending on your requirement.

In store for Equity Investors / Property Owners

HDS provides investors with Return on Investment analysis, cashflow projections and also Market Value analysis. Investment analysis and reporting package will provide investors with various scenarios, including the Debt / Equity models to analyse the returns. The investor will be able to decide on the capital structure that would bring in the expected returns.

Investors will also be provided with quarterly cashflow analysis of the property to understand the income generation capability of the property invested.

Prepare Annual Budgets and Quarterly Reforecasting using Client ERP system and Reporting accordingly.

In store for Lenders / Financial Institutions:

Interactive Loan Sizing (ILS) is an online platform for the lenders (Banks / Financial Institutions) to process a loan, facilitating a real-time interaction between the lenders and the borrower (Individuals / Corporate).

Advantages for ILS are

  • Quick availability of documents
  • Reduces Communication & delivery time
  • Reduces time spent on Loan Sizing and Analysis

ILS and the data team together can reduce the overall commercial loan cycle by 40% and the overall cost by 50%.
