  • Acquisition - Complete due diligence on the property including

Asset Management

Asset Management is vast term and involves multiple activities on a consistent basis. HDS is equipped to provide any or all of the below services to support Asset Management Clients.

Lease Abstraction

This service can be done at any point during the life cycle of an asset

Valuation Support

Typically, all Asset Management companies would keep track of their assets by looking at the cash flows on a regular interval (Monthly / Quarterly). Also, they would keep their books updated with the Asset value, appraised by a Third Party Appraiser, again on any regular interval (mostly quarterly, or annual).

Argus Modelling, Audit & Updates

HDS will create a new Argus DCF / AE model based on the source documents and assumptions provided.

HDS will verify the existing Argus model (generally the seller’s / broker’s model) with the Lease Agreements for accuracy of the model.

HDS will update the existing Argus model for leasing activity or Financials update. Updating the Argus model for Leasing Assumptions will also be part of the scope of work.

Hold / Sell analysis

During regular reviews, Asset Managers will choose to hold a particular property, or sell a particular property based on its performance. The model will provide the Asset Managers with IRR at different points in time, starting from the time of acquisition of the asset, until going forward for the next 5 years from the analysis.

Budgeting & Reforecasting

Prepare Annual Budgets and Quarterly Reforecasting using Client ERP system and Reporting accordingly.

Acquisition Support

This is a financial model in Excel. This will help the lender to size the loan amount for the particular property in question.

Origination Underwriting

Underwriting for a loan once the loan amount is finalized. This is a complete underwriting based on guidelines provided by the bank / financial institution. The outcome of this underwriting is to determine the NOI, NCF, DSC and LTV at the time of origination of the loan.

Asset Summary

Asset Summary is word document that contains information on the property in /to-be mortgage. This is preferred report which banks / financial institutions would like to on every asset that a loan is issued on.
We can attain basic information from the bank, and source other information from authorized websites and prepare the summary.

Securitization

Re-underwriting

This is a miniature underwriting performed based on guidelines provided by the Issuer of the bond (typically, a separate division in a bank / financial institution). Again, this is to ensure the NOI, NCF, Value, DSC and LTV - overall performance of the loan, based on performance of the property.
Based on this result, issuer decides the Exit strategy for the loan (Defeasance or Yield Maintenance).

Financial Statement Analysis

This is also called the Operating Statement Analysis Report (OSAR), where on a regular basis (monthly or quarterly), the financial statements of the loans (indirectly, the financial statements of the properties) will be analyzed to arrive at the monthly / quarterly DSC and LTV.
This is a very specific service that we will provide to the Servicers involved in the Securitization process.

Asset Summary

Word document that contains information on the property associated with the loan that is securitized.
